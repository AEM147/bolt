# Visual Design and Brand Guidelines

## 1. Character Design

**Concept:** A sleek and modern robot with a friendly and expressive face.

**Key Features:**
- **Head:** A large, circular head with a glowing LED face that can display a wide range of emotions and expressions. The face can also show data visualizations and graphics.
- **Body:** A compact and agile body with a white and cobalt blue color scheme. The body should have a clean, minimalist design with smooth, rounded edges.
- **Limbs:** Articulated limbs with five-fingered hands that allow for expressive gestures.
- **Logo:** A stylized "B" in the shape of a lightning bolt, prominently displayed on the chest.

**Inspiration:**
- **Eve from WALL-E:** For her sleek, minimalist design and expressive eyes.
- **Baymax from Big Hero 6:** For his friendly and approachable demeanor.
- **The Portal Turrets from Portal:** For their clean, futuristic aesthetic and witty personalities.

## 2. Brand Guidelines

### Color Palette

- **Primary Colors:**
  - **Cobalt Blue:** #0047AB (for the main body and accents)
  - **White:** #FFFFFF (for the main body)
- **Secondary Colors:**
  - **Electric Yellow:** #FFFF00 (for highlights, calls to action, and the logo)
  - **Charcoal Gray:** #36454F (for text and backgrounds)

### Typography

- **Primary Font:** **"Roboto"** - A clean and modern sans-serif font that is highly readable on screens.
  - **Headlines:** Roboto Bold
  - **Body Text:** Roboto Regular
- **Secondary Font:** **"Source Code Pro"** - A monospaced font that can be used for code snippets and technical information to give a more "techy" feel.

### Visual Style

- **Overall Aesthetic:** Clean, minimalist, and futuristic.
- **Graphics:** Use a combination of 2D and 3D graphics. The 2D graphics should be simple and icon-based, while the 3D graphics should be high-quality and photorealistic.
- **Motion Graphics:** Use smooth and dynamic animations to keep the content engaging.

## 3. Templates

### Thumbnail Template

- **Layout:** A close-up of Bolt's face on one side, with a bold, attention-grabbing headline on the other.
- **Branding:** Include the channel logo and a consistent color scheme.

### Logo

- **Primary Logo:** The stylized "B" lightning bolt.
- **Secondary Logo:** The full name "Bolt" written in Roboto Bold.

### Brand Assets

- **Lower Thirds:** A simple animation that displays the channel name and social media handles.
- **End Screens:** A call to action to subscribe, watch more videos, and follow on social media.
