# Script Templates for Bolt

Here are the script templates for the four content pillars of Bolt's channel. Each template includes a hook, main content section, and a call to action (CTA).

## 1. AI News Updates

**Hook:** "You won't believe what just happened in the world of AI! 🤯"

**Main Content:**
- **What's the news?** (e.g., "OpenAI just announced...")
- **Why does it matter?** (e.g., "This could change the way we...")
- **What's the key takeaway?** (e.g., "The bottom line is...")

**CTA:** "What do you think about this? Let me know in the comments below! And don't forget to follow for more daily AI news! #AINews #TechUpdate #BoltAI"

## 2. AI Tool of the Day

**Hook:** "This AI tool is a game-changer for [target audience]! 🚀"

**Main Content:**
- **What is the tool?** (e.g., "It's called [Tool Name] and it helps you...")
- **How does it work?** (Show a quick demo of the tool in action)
- **Why is it useful?** (e.g., "It can save you hours of work by...")

**CTA:** "Have you tried this tool yet? Let me know what you think! And follow for more awesome AI tool recommendations! #AITools #ProductivityHacks #BoltAI"

## 3. AI Concepts Explained

**Hook:** "Ever wondered how [AI concept] actually works? Let's break it down in 60 seconds! ⏱️"

**Main Content:**
- **What is the concept?** (Provide a simple, clear definition)
- **How does it work?** (Use an analogy or a simple example to explain the concept)
- **Why should you care?** (Explain the real-world implications of the concept)

**CTA:** "Did that make sense? Let me know if you have any questions! And be sure to follow for more AI explained! #AIExplained #LearnAI #BoltAI"

## 4. AI in Daily Life

**Hook:** "Did you know you're probably using AI every day without even realizing it? 🤔"

**Main Content:**
- **Where do you use AI?** (e.g., "From your Netflix recommendations to your Google Maps route...")
- **How does it work in this context?** (Briefly explain the AI behind the scenes)
- **What are the benefits?** (e.g., "This makes your life easier by...")

**CTA:** "What other examples of AI in daily life can you think of? Share them in the comments! And follow for more insights into the world of AI! #AIinRealLife #TechInOurLives #BoltAI"
