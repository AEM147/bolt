# Research Plan: Content Strategy & Automation Pipeline

## Objectives
- Identify and catalog 15+ reliable AI news sources with RSS feeds/APIs.
- Create an automated news monitoring and filtering system for US-relevant content.
- Develop script templates for all 4 content pillars (News, Tools, Concepts, Daily Life).
- Design a 30-day rotating content calendar with optimal posting schedules.
- Create platform-specific optimization strategies (YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels).
- Develop hashtag strategies and content hooks for maximum engagement.
- Build a content quality scoring and trending topic identification system.
- Create backup content generation for slow news days.

## Research Breakdown
- **News Source Identification**:
  - Find and list top AI and tech news websites.
  - Verify the availability of RSS feeds or APIs for each source.
- **Competitor and Platform Analysis**:
  - Analyze content strategies of successful AI/tech creators on YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels.
  - Research optimal posting times, content formats, and hashtag strategies for each platform targeting a US audience.
- **Automation Tool Research**:
  - Investigate free and low-cost tools for news aggregation, content scheduling, and social media management.
- **Trending Topic Analysis**:
  - Study current and recurring trending topics within the AI and tech communities.
  - Analyze news cycles and how they can be leveraged for content creation.

## Key Questions
1. What are the most reliable and timely sources for AI news?
2. What content formats and topics generate the most engagement on short-form video platforms for a tech-savvy US audience?
3. What are the best practices for optimizing content (titles, descriptions, hashtags) on YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels?
4. What are the most effective and efficient ways to automate the content sourcing and creation process?
5. How can we develop a system to consistently measure content quality and identify trending topics?

## Resource Strategy
- **Primary Tools:** `batch_web_search` for identifying news sources, competitor analysis, and researching automation tools. Python scripting for developing the automation pipeline.
- **Search Strategies:** Use keywords like "top AI news sources RSS feed," "best tech news APIs," "AI content creator strategy TikTok," "YouTube Shorts algorithm," "Instagram Reels engagement tips."

## Verification Plan
- **Source Reliability:** Cross-reference news sources against multiple lists and community recommendations.
- **Tool Viability:** Test the identified automation tools to ensure they meet the project's requirements.
- **Strategy Effectiveness:** A/B test different content formats, posting times, and hashtag strategies once the pipeline is operational.

## Expected Deliverables
- A cataloged list of AI news sources with RSS feeds/APIs.
- An automated news aggregation and filtering script.
- A set of script templates for different content pillars.
- A 30-day content calendar.
- A document outlining platform-specific optimization and hashtag strategies.
- A conceptual design for a content quality scoring system.
- A contingency plan for content generation.
- A final report and all associated files.

## Workflow Selection
- **Primary focus:** Search & Development
- **Justification:** This task requires a combination of in-depth research to inform the strategy and development work to build the automation pipeline. I will start with research and then move to development.
