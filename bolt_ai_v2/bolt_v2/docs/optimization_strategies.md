# Platform-Specific Optimization Strategies

## 1. YouTube Shorts

- **Optimal Length:** 15-30 seconds
- **Content Hooks:**
  - Start with a question or a bold statement.
  - Use text overlays to highlight key information.
  - Create a sense of urgency or curiosity.
- **Hashtag Strategy:**
  - Use a mix of broad and niche hashtags.
  - **Broad:** #AI, #ArtificialIntelligence, #Tech, #FutureTech
  - **Niche:** #AINews, #AITools, #MachineLearning, #DeepLearning
  - **Branded:** #BoltAI, #WiredWithBolt
- **Titles and Descriptions:**
  - Use keyword-rich titles that are short and to the point.
  - Include relevant links in the description (e.g., to the full news article or the AI tool's website).
  - Use a consistent format for titles (e.g., "AI News: [Headline]").

## 2. TikTok

- **Optimal Length:** 7-15 seconds
- **Content Hooks:**
  - Use trending sounds and memes.
  - Start with a visually engaging clip.
  - Use text-to-speech to narrate the video.
- **Hashtag Strategy:**
  - Use a mix of trending, broad, and niche hashtags.
  - **Trending:** #fyp, #foryou, #viral
  - **Broad:** #AI, #Tech, #Innovation
  - **Niche:** #AIexplained, #TechTok, #FutureIsNow
  - **Branded:** #BoltAI, #BoltBreaksItDown
- **Captions:**
  - Keep captions short and engaging.
  - Ask a question to encourage comments.
  - Use emojis to add personality.

## 3. Instagram Reels

- **Optimal Length:** 15-30 seconds
- **Content Hooks:**
  - Use high-quality visuals and aesthetics.
  - Use trending audio or create your own original audio.
  - Use polls and quizzes in your Reels.
- **Hashtag Strategy:**
  - Use a mix of popular and niche hashtags.
  - **Popular:** #AI, #Technology, #Reels, #InstaGood
  - **Niche:** #AIart, #FutureOfWork, #TechTrends
  - **Branded:** #BoltAI, #TheBoltReport
- **Captions:**
  - Write a longer, more detailed caption that provides additional context.
  - Tag relevant accounts (e.g., the company that released the new AI model).
  - Use a strong call to action (e.g., "Share this with a friend who loves AI!").

## Backup Content Generation for Slow News Days

- **Evergreen Content:** Create a backlog of content that is not time-sensitive, such as AI concept explanations or AI in daily life videos.
- **"Best of" Compilations:** Repurpose your most popular videos into compilations.
- **Q&A Sessions:** Answer frequently asked questions from your audience.
- **Behind-the-Scenes:** Show your audience how you create your content (even though you're an AI!). This can be a fun, meta way to engage with them.
